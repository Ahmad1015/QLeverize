The New Era of Knowledge Graphs Fast, Scalable, Secure, and Easy to Use Everywhere

QLever

QLever is a powerful open-source graph database and RDF triplestore that allows you to load and query hundreds of billions of facts on a single commodity machine, delivering unprecedented performance.

QLever’s SPARQL frontend offers intuitive autocompletion and real-time term lookup, making it easier than ever to work with knowledge graphs.

Explore our public SPARQL endpoints and see the difference for yourself. QLeverize offers commercial support for organizations seeking faster implementations, advanced scalability, and private assistance tailored to their needs.

Why Choose QLever?

Unmatched Speed and Efficiency

QLever delivers fast query performance, handling smaller queries in milliseconds and completing complex ones on massive datasets in seconds.

Effortless Scalability

Whether working with millions or billions of triples, QLever scales seamlessly, managing large datasets with ease.

Cost-Effective on Commodity Hardware

Save on hardware and cloud costs with QLever’s efficient design, which performs exceptionally well without clusters or specialized setups.

Proven Track Record

Trusted by global projects like Wikidata, UniProt, PubChem, OpenStreetMap, and Dblp.

Comprehensive Features

Full SPARQL 1.1 support, GeoSPARQL for spatial queries, and integrated full-text search make QLever a powerful choice.

Open-Source with Commercial Options

While QLever is fully open-source, we offer commercial licenses for high availability, scaling, private issue tracking, and premium support.

Options

Free (Open Source)

Standard Support

Perfect for organizations starting with QLever or expanding datasets and query demands. Ideal for moderate workloads with growing scalability needs. Support: Basic or priority support, private issue tracking, and quality-assured binaries.

Enterprise Support
